Monthly Meetups

We are a passionate community dedicated to preserving the legacy of AJS & Matchless motorcycles, celebrating the heritage of British engineering that has stood the test of time. Nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Devon and Cornwall, our club offers a place where enthusiasts, restorers, and riders alike can come together to share their love for these iconic machines.

Whether you are a seasoned rider or a curious newcomer, our doors are open. Join us as we ride through the history of motorcycling, participate in thrilling events, and keep the spirit of AJS & Matchless alive for future generations. Explore our site to stay updated on upcoming events, discover useful resources, and connect with fellow members of the AJS & Matchless community.
